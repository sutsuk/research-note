# Emphasis Filter

## 1. Introduction

- Communication line is assumed Low-Pass Filter (LPF).
- Pre-Emphasis Filter and De-Emphasis Filter make up for the such effect.

## 2. Pre-Emphasis

$$
Z_c = \frac{1}{j \cdot 2\pi \cdot f \cdot C}

j: Imaginary unit
f: Input frequency
C: Capacitance [F]
$$
